
# 🎯 Order Analytics Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview

This project analyzes **1,000 orders** from a gift and occasion-based e-commerce business. The dashboard provides actionable insights into revenue, product performance, delivery efficiency, and customer behavior to support strategic decision-making.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

- ✅ **Total Orders** — 1,000 orders tracked
- ✅ **Revenue Analysis** — ₹6,74,634 total revenue
- ✅ **Delivery Performance** — Average 5.52 days delivery time
- ✅ **Customer Spending** — ₹3,290.90 average per order
- ✅ **Top Products** — Gift packs dominate revenue
- ✅ **Category Analysis** — Cakes, sweets, and soft toys lead
- ✅ **Occasion-Based Insights** — Raksha Bandhan, Valentine's Day, Holi
- ✅ **City-Wise Revenue** — Geographic performance breakdown

## 📊 Revenue Breakdown

### By Category
- 🎂 Cake — Highest revenue
- 🍬 Sweets — Strong second
- 🧸 Soft Toys — Consistent performer
- 🌿 Plants — Niche but growing
- 🎨 Colors — Seasonal (Holi-driven)
- 🎁 Raksha Bandhan — Occasion-specific spike

### By Month
Revenue peaks during festive months (Raksha Bandhan, Holi, Valentine's Day). Off-peak months could benefit from targeted promotions.

### By City
Top-performing cities include **Aby**, **Amud**, **Caltex**, and **Dhamdha**. Revenue concentration indicates where to focus marketing spend.

4. Explore insights in the summary sheets
